Overview

Purpose

[Are you tired of this war between the Imperials and the terrorist rebel scum? What can you do to help your great Emperor bring peace and prosperity to galaxy? This website will bring enough information and recourses needed to start your great journey with the great Imperial Army. I will highlight key job opportunities needed, and what requirements are necessary to join. I will also highlight the grand benefits that come with service, and what you can do with the skills you learn after service. I have made many recruitment websites, as I am an Imperial Engineer and have worked on many projects.]

Audience

[The intended audience for this website is young adults from a wide variety of races across the galaxy. Fit and able body individuals who want to make a difference and who want to feel like they are a part of a team. 18–30 year old’s, who maybe have no other options, or feel a since of duty to the Empire, or who have been hurt/wronged by the Republic. It will also educate the older citizens of the Empire that we mean business when we are recruiting and that we are professional and highly capable of producing a top tier army to protect them.]

Content

Home page

[The enlistment process will the on the home page, detailing how to enlist and where to go. It will also detail the requirements that will need to be met. The people coming will see a general overview of what the empire is looking for and if they qualify for the process. It will highlight a signing bonus, and give some propaganda against the Rebellion. It will answer some basic questions the visitors are asking, and it will redirect them to the following two pages to answer more in-depth questions. It will have the Empires moto on it, have a contact us information at the bottom, with a live chat option, recruiters near you option, and the com frequency to speak to one over the “phone”.]

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

[This next page will talk about the different benefits of service that the Empire has. It will state the different skills that you will learn as a trooper, officer, pilot, or any other profession. It will go into retirement plans, lengths of service, and how your family will be taken care of if anything should happen to you while you are fighting. It will tell of how you are helping protect the galaxy from people who are trying to disturb the peace and how you are able to bring about justice and retribution to those people who have hurt so many others of this great empire. It will lay out in detail the different types of incurrences that are quarantined with service, and how to rank up to be an officer or continue to be an enlisted soldier.]

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

[This final page will be about all the different type of careers that the Empire offers in the military, whither it be a regular storm trooper, a tie pilot, a shock trooper, or even a bridge officer. It will lay out pay and benefits of each station, and how much risk each has. It will also tell of lengths of deployment, which planets and sectors one should expect to see/be in, and how needed each position is. It will also detail what to expect in each position, and how each helps the Empire. It will also talk about the reserve units, and will talk about the different military academy’s that are throughout the galaxy that train in these different careers.]
